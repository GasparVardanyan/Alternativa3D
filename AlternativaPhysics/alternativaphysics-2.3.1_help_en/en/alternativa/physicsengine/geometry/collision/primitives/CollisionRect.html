Classpublic class CollisionRect
InheritanceCollisionRect CollisionConvex CollisionPrimitive PhysicsEventDispatcher alternativa.physicsengine.events.PhysicsEventRegistryDispatcher

Oriented rectangle. It is given by half the size along the axis X (width) and Y (length) of the local coordinate system of the primitive. Thus, the rectangle is in the plane XY, its sides parallel to the axes of the plane and the normal is directed along the local axis Z.
