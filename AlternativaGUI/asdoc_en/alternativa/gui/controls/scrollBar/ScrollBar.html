Packagealternativa.gui.controls.scrollBar
Classpublic class ScrollBar
InheritanceScrollBar GUIobject flash.display.Sprite

If data don't fit on the screen user can manage the viewing part of data with the ScrollBar component. The scroll bar consists of four parts: two buttons with the arrows, track and roller. Position of the roller and buttons mapping depend of current stage of the scroll bar.
