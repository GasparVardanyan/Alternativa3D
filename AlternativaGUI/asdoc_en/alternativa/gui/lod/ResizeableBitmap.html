Classpublic class ResizeableBitmap
InheritanceResizeableBitmap GUIobject flash.display.Sprite
SubclassesDiscretResizeableBitmap

An object that represents the texture in the form of a sequence of small copies. Each of the following two times smaller than the previous one. The latter has a size of 1x1 pixels. The smaller the width of the object is rendered, the smaller the texture is selected. This allows you to get a better visual outcome and greater productivity.
